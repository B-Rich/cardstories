How to play

  • The Game Master picks a card...

    1.

  • ...and creates a story for it.

    2.
    "My new haircut has changed me."

  • That's the Card Story!

    3.

  • Without seeing the Card Story, each player chooses one fake card based on the story that was created.
    *You'll need 3-6 players!

    4.

  • All the cards are shuffled,
    including the Card Stories.

    5.

  • You win if you prove that you can find THE CARD STORY!

    6.
